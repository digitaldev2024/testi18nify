Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about i18nify

What types of projects can I translate?

i18nify supports web projects (React, Vue, Angular, Next.js, etc.), mobile applications (React Native, Flutter) and any project containing translation files (JSON, YAML, etc.).

How does automatic translation work?

Our specialized AI analyzes your code to understand the context before generating translations. You can then adjust them manually if necessary.

Can I collaborate with my team?

Yes, Pro and Team plans allow you to invite team members to work together on translations with roles and permissions.

What is the quality of the translations?

Our translations are more accurate than generic solutions thanks to contextual analysis. For critical projects, we recommend human review.

How does the free trial work?

The free trial gives you access to all Pro features for 14 days, with no commitment.

Can I cancel at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time without fees or conditions.

Our mission

i18nify was born from the founders' frustration with existing internationalization tools. As former developers, we know how time-consuming and error-prone translation management can be.

Our goal is to provide a solution that seamlessly integrates the translation process into the development workflow, without sacrificing quality or control.
